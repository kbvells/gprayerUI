Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Your Privacy is Our Priority: Bhakthi Padalgal does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Your spiritual practice remains completely private.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bhakthi Padalgal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your spiritual practice remains completely private. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data collection and your rights.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

Bhakthi Padalgal is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or browsing history
  • Reading habits or preferences
  • Search queries within the app
  • Any other personal or behavioral data

3. Data Storage

All app content and your preferences are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Devotional song lyrics and translations
  • Your reading preferences (font size, theme, language)
  • Premium subscription status (managed by Apple)

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

If you choose to purchase a premium subscription (¬£4.99/year), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive only:

  • Confirmation that you have an active subscription
  • No payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)
  • No personal identification information

Your subscription is managed through your Apple ID. All payment data and personal information is handled exclusively by Apple according to their privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Bhakthi Padalgal does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics tools
  • Facebook SDK or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services

6. Children's Privacy

Bhakthi Padalgal is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any data, there are no special privacy concerns for children using the app. Parents can be confident that their children's privacy is fully protected.

7. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect any information, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information from our servers (as we don't have any servers storing your data).

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations, you have various rights regarding your data. Since we don't collect any data, these rights are automatically fulfilled:

  • Right to Access: All your data is on your device, accessible to you at any time
  • Right to Deletion: Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
  • Right to Portability: Your data never leaves your device
  • Right to Object: No data collection means nothing to object to

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: kb_vells@proton.me
  • Developer: Vel, London, United Kingdom

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • UK Data Protection Act 2018 - United Kingdom
  • Apple App Store Privacy Guidelines

12. Consent

By using Bhakthi Padalgal, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since we don't collect any data, your use of the app does not result in any data processing that requires additional consent.

Summary: We believe your spiritual practice is deeply personal. That's why Bhakthi Padalgal collects zero data. Everything stays on your device, and your privacy is always protected.
