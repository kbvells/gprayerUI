Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Introduction

We respect your privacy in the Bhakthi Padalgal app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the app and how we use it.

Access to Files (User-Granted)

iOS: This app may request access to iCloud Drive or local files solely so that you can import an audio file for personal use.

Android: This app may request access to Google Drive, Dropbox, or local files solely so that you can import an audio file for personal use.

This permission is entirely user-granted - it is requested only when you choose to continue. The app accesses only the specific audio file you select; it does not access, scan, or upload any other files or folders. The app does not bundle or stream songs by default; you may load your own audio file at your discretion.

Data We Do NOT Collect (Explicit)

We do NOT collect the following personal information:

  • Your name, address, or phone number
  • Location information (GPS/precise location)
  • Contacts/photos/camera content
  • User-generated content

Note: On the "Contact Us" page you may optionally provide your email address. This is entirely optional.

Information We Collect

We collect only minimal, privacy-friendly data:

  • Screen/feature usage events (for example settings usage, Contact Us/Privacy Policy opens, Read Verse/Load Songs/Meaning taps, and subscription access source)
  • Technical context for subscription purchases (device model, iOS version, app version)
  • Crash/diagnostic reports
  • Contact Us (bug report): device model, iOS version, app version
  • Contact Us: optional email if you choose to provide it

We do NOT collect personal identifiers, precise location, contacts, or user content.

How We Use Information

Collected minimal usage/diagnostic data is used to:

  • Understand feature usage patterns
  • Improve subscription flow experience (including source screens)
  • Diagnose and fix issues/crashes
  • Improve app stability and reliability

This information:

  • Is NOT shared with third parties
  • Is NOT used for ad targeting
  • Is NOT sold or rented

Crash Reports and Diagnostics

We use Apple MetricKit-based crash/diagnostic monitoring to improve reliability. For crash events, technical context such as reason, device model, iOS version, and app version may be recorded for debugging purposes.

Contact Us and Bug Reports

When you send a bug report via the "Contact Us" page, we include only minimal device details:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15)
  • iOS version
  • App version (Version & Build)
  • Language/theme/font size preferences
  • Email address - only if you choose to provide it (optional)

This information is used solely to analyze and resolve issues; it is not shared with third parties.

Note: For Feature Request/Improvement, you may provide your email address only if you choose to (optional).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your usage data. All data is stored and transmitted securely.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Stop data collection by deleting the app
  • Ask questions about this privacy policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support section.
